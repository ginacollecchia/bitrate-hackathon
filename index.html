SCROLLHOLE is a scrolling interface for making sound, designed by Gina Collecchia and Eric Heep. Scroll up and down to travel through the hole. Scroll long enough, fast enough, and you WILL be rewarded.

Submitted for the 2020 BitRate Hackathon, sponsored by Gray Area and Google. Made with Tone.js, P5.js, and Magenta.js.

Did you find a BUG? Submit an issue at github.com/ginacollecchia/scrollhole. Special thanks to Myles Borins for help with Node shtuff!
